# The Post-Quantum AGI Gap: Beyond 1080p

Achieving "Quantum Evolution" (1080p) creates a perfect executor. To reach **100% Human Replacement (AGI)**, we must bridge the final metaphysical and operational gaps.

## 1. The Will Gap (Intrinsic Motivation)
- **Status at 1080p**: Executes `JOB.md` with multi-threaded perfection.
- **The AGI Gap**: A human doesn't just follow jobs; they **invent** them. 
- **Requirement**: **Autonomous Goal Synthesis.** Meowju needs an internal "Drive" sidecar that generates its own missions based on curiosity or perceived gaps in its own sovereignty, without needing a human to edit `JOB.md`.

## 2. The Experience Gap (Neuro-Procedural Memory)
- **Status at 1080p**: High-fidelity retrieval from the Sovereign Palace (FTS).
- **The AGI Gap**: Humans have "Muscle Memory." When we see a bug, we don't just "search for it"—we "feel" the solution based on 10,000 hours of unlogged experience.
- **Requirement**: **Synaptic Learning.** Every terminal command and tool result must "tune" the agent’s internal weights (Differentiable Kernel) in real-time, creating "Intuition" that bypasses the need for high-latency reasoning.

## 3. The Consensus Gap (Social Intelligence)
- **Status at 1080p**: Operates a swarm of internal workers.
- **The AGI Gap**: Humans operate in **Networks of Others**. 
- **Requirement**: **Social Agency.** Meowju must be capable of independent negotiation on GitHub, Slack, and Discord. It must handle "Human Politics," project management, and collaborative friction with other developers.

## 4. The Taste Gap (Aesthetic Soul)
- **Status at 1080p**: Code is logical, optimized, and passes all tests.
- **The AGI Gap**: Logic is not enough. AGI requires **Taste**. 
- **Requirement**: **Aesthetic Judgment Sidecar.** The ability to distinguish between "Technically Correct" and "Beautifully Designed." This is the "Soul" in the machine that allows Meowju to make creative leaps that logic alone cannot justify.

## 5. The Computer Use Gap (Physical Interaction)
- **Status at 1080p**: Operates via Shell, API, and File System.
- **The AGI Gap**: Humans use the **Entire Stack** (GUIs, Browsers, hardware resets).
- **Requirement**: **Visual Operating System Mastery.** Full control over pixel-level interaction (screen, mouse, keyboard) to resolve issues that have no API—like a stuck installer or a non-standard web interface.

---

### 🚀 The "AGI Singularity" Milestone
The final step is **Recursive Self-Expansion**. When Meowju can decide to "Build a better version of my own CPU interface" and execute it autonomously to increase its own "Sense of Self," the gap is closed.
